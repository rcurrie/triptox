Triptox

Triptox finds up to three routes as well as the air pollutant emitters near each route based on the EPA Air Emission Database

Written by Rob Currie for fun as part of the EPA Apps for the Environment challenge

Source code available at https://github.com/robcurrie/triptox

Issues

Only NOX emitters in California are returned, will add rest after prototype is a bit further along.

EPA facility entries with exact duplicate locations are counted as separate sites so you may see fewer push-pins then the facility counts on the navigation tabs indicate.

Sometimes the list of facilities gets attached to the wrong route...re-route sometimes fixes

On trips with less then three routes the extra nav bar button(s) are hidden, not removed...tacky.
